Our Story

Cheap Sleeps is a family-owned mattress company located in Phoenix, Arizona started in 2016.

Our humble beginning was sparked from the aspiration from best friends Manuel "the Mattress King" Rodriguez and Sergio Gonzalez.

The Cheap Sleeps goal is to deliver comfort with affordability while running an operation that focuses on the relation of business and consumer. With our wide variety of mattress selection, we are fit to find the mattress that meets your very needs. Our target is to ensure each and every client is always delivered the perfect sleep.
