Keanu Charles Reeves is an actor, director, producer, and musician. Keanu, whose first name means "cool breeze over the mountains" in Hawaiian, was born September 2, 1964 in Beirut, Lebanon. Keanu is son of Patricia, a costume designer, and Samuel Nowlin Reeves, a geoligist. His mother was Enlish born while his father was born in Hawaii, of British, Portuguese, Native Hawaiin, and Chinese ancestry. Although born in Lebanon, Keanu is a Canadian citizen.

Keanu's life has been defaced by sorrow and grief. When Keanu was three, his father left the family. He was incarcerated for selling drugs at a Hawaiian airport. After the divorce, his family moved from city to city where he kept changing high schools. In 1993, at the age of 23, River Phoenix passed away of a drug overdose. Keanu lost his bestfriend. In 1999 Keanu and Jennifer Syme were expecting a child, but tragedy unfolded when the baby was stillborn. 18 months later Syme would die in a car accident. Even though experiencing much agony and despite the setbacks, he still pushes forward.

"Grief changes shape, but it never ends" - Keanu Reeves

Keanu is known for being the gold-hearted man some of us wish to be. It is rumored Keanu gave away an estimated $80 million of his $114 million earnings from the Matrix trilogy. He also willingly took paycuts from films like the Replacements and the Devil's Advocates to hire actors like Gene Hackman and Al Pacino to keep casting within a budget. After his sister was diagnosed with leukimia, he donated to a vast amount of money to cancer charities, research centers, and hospitals. Keanu has also set up his own chairty for leukemia but does not attach his name to it. He is known for his huge support for other charities like Stand Up to Cancer and Sick Kids Foundation.
